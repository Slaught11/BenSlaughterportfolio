"Immersive Relaxation Garden:"

The "Immersive Relaxation Garden" is a dome-based project I undertook during my second year of my Digital Media Design degree.

The project features an immersive first-person visual tour of an environment built for the main purpose of bringing a calming experience.

This piece was built through Unreal Engine Game development software. To make the experience I paired level design with keyframe animation to bring a high level of immersion.
